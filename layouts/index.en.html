Cultural Narrative of a City

This project is presenting various Cultural Narratives of an urban city, by connecting cultural resources past and present, and scholarly research on culture. This website collects and shares references for everyone to create their own narratives. Cards share overviews of local cultural institutions and resources. Projects and events introduce events and activities based on cultural resources. Notes further explain the contents of Cards and Projects.
